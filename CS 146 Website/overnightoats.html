Instructions:

  1. Combine 1/2 cup rolled oats and 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based) in a jar.
  2. Add sweetener (like honey or maple syrup), a pinch of salt, and your favorite toppings (fruits, nuts, seeds).
  3. Stir well, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  4. In the morning, give it a good mix, and your creamy overnight oats are ready to enjoy!
